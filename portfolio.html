Event

Problem

When we experience a significant event in our lives, we want to preserve the memory of it for ourselves and future generations. One such event was the Ruracio, a traditional ceremony where the groom’s family pays the bride price to the bride’s family as a sign of respect and gratitude. The challenge was how to document this event in a meaningful and authentic way.

Solution

To document this event in a meaningful and authentic way, we captured the whole ceremony on video and edited it into a two-minute highlight reel that showcased the most important moments of the event. We used music, pacing, and relevant cuts to create a captivating and emotional narrative that conveyed the purpose and significance of the Ruracio.

Testimonial

Problem

The client wanted to showcase testimonials in their new website to demonstrate the value and quality of their course.They wanted to feature real stories from satisfied customers who had positive experiences with their brand. The client hoped that by displaying testimonials in their new website, they would increase trust, loyalty, and conversions among their potential and existing customers.

Solution

They wanted to showcase testimonials in their new website in a way that would appeal to their target audience. They contacted some of their previous students who had successfully completed the course and asked them to share their stories. We recorded their testimonials and edited them in an engaging and captivating way for the website. We used music, transitions, b-roll, and graphics to enhance the quality and impact of the testimonials. We also added their names, locations, and photos to make them more credible and relatable.

Advert

Problem

The client had partnered with an interior design company to offer a comprehensive course that covers both landscaping and interior design. They wanted to create an advert that would feature both CEOs talking about the benefits of the course. They wanted to highlight how the course would teach students the skills and knowledge needed to design beautiful and functional spaces, both indoors and outdoors. They also wanted to showcase some of the projects and testimonials from previous students who had successfully completed the course.

Solution

We wanted to create an advert that would feature both CEOs in a professional and appealing way. We recorded each CEO separately in different locations that matched their brand identity. We then edited the videos in post-production to create a seamless transition from one CEO to the other. We also added music, graphics, and b-rolls to enhance the quality and impact of the advert. The final result was a compelling video that showcased why their target audience should enroll in this course.

Youtube

Problem(Sample Video)

One of the challenges of video editing is to make a talking head video more engaging and dynamic. A talking head video is a video where a person speaks directly to the camera, usually with a single camera angle and minimal movement. While this type of video can be effective for delivering information or opinions, it can also be boring and monotonous for the viewers.

Solution

To solve this problem, I used video editing techniques to transform a talking head video into a more captivating and lively video. I added zooms, motion graphics, b-roll, and sound effects to enhance the overall experience of the video.
